Step 1 — Connect Your Domain

Point midwestern.construction (registered through Squarespace) to your Vercel site. You need to make changes in both Vercel and Squarespace.

In Vercel:

1
Log into vercel.com → click your project.
2
Go to Settings → Domains.
3
Type midwestern.construction → click Add.
4
Also add www.midwestern.construction → click Add.
5
Vercel will show a red warning with DNS records — use the values in the table below.

In Squarespace:

1
Log into squarespace.com → click Domains in the left menu.
2
Click midwestern.construction → click DNS Settings.
3
Scroll down to Custom Records → add these two records:
TypeName / HostValue
A@76.76.21.21
CNAMEwwwcname.vercel-dns.com
4
Save the records. Go back to Vercel — once DNS propagates it will show a green checkmark next to your domain.
ℹ️ DNS changes can take anywhere from a few minutes to 24 hours to go live. You do not need to change nameservers — just add the two records above.
⚠ Note: If your current Squarespace website is using this domain, changing these DNS records will take it offline and point visitors to your new site instead. Make sure you're ready to go live before doing this.

Step 2 — Activate the Contact Form

The contact form is built and working. Currently it sends test emails. To receive real submissions at support@midwestern.construction, complete the steps below.

Verify your domain on Resend:

1
Go to resend.com → log in → click Domains → click Add Domain.
2
Type midwestern.construction → click Add. Resend will display a list of TXT and MX records.
3
Go back to Squarespace DNS Settings (same place as Step 1) and add the records below under Custom Records:
TypeName / HostValuePriority
TXT resend._domainkey p=MIGfMA0GCSqGSIb3DQEBAQUAA4GNADCBiQKBgQDWDYGsMvFkstDXdlIm2Q8i23hAeW+1C4V2xOSq7XzKZ8VvRalsJV+VKthyOpLgAbOBKis2ZLyMSk9jbN4HcDaxOyu8cL4xvShaPMYsUHGL/i/m3CbmlAG3fwpNlEhqy5Hu683fwGeyDSBnLBFigUtVk0SR/bRQ2YCKRrG6kN0T4wIDAQAB —
MX send feedback-smtp.us-east-1.amazonses.com 10
TXT send v=spf1 include:amazonses.com ~all —
TXT _dmarc v=DMARC1; p=none; —
ℹ️ The DMARC record is optional but recommended — it helps prevent your emails from being marked as spam.
4
Return to Resend → click Verify Domain. Usually confirms within a few minutes.
5
Let your developer know — they'll update one line of code to switch the form to send real emails to support@midwestern.construction.
✓ Once active, every contact form submission will arrive in your inbox with the customer's name, phone number, email, service needed, and message. You can reply directly from your email.

Step 3 — Google Ads & Analytics Pending your IDs

To track ad performance and website visitors, your developer needs two IDs from your Google account. No login sharing required — just the ID numbers.

Find your Google Analytics ID:

1
Go to analytics.google.com → click your property → Admin → Data Streams.
2
Click your web stream — the Measurement ID is shown at the top right. It looks like: G-XXXXXXXXXX

Find your Google Ads Conversion ID:

1
Go to ads.google.com → Tools → Measurement → Conversions.
2
Click a conversion action → Tag setup. The Conversion ID looks like: AW-XXXXXXXXXX
Google Analytics IDG- _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
Google Ads IDAW- _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Once you have these, send them to your developer. They'll add the tracking snippets to every page of the site — no further action needed from you.
